ThunderRun Rafting Co.

At ThunderRun Rafting, we chase the roar of white water so you can find yours. Our mission is simple: deliver heart-pounding adventure without cutting corners on safety. Every trip is led by expert guides who treat the river with respect and every paddler like family. We build courage through teamwork, protect the wild waters we run, and leave every guest with a story worth telling. Not just a ride. A rumble.

History

ThunderRun Rafting was founded in 1995 by Jake "Thunder" Marston, a wilderness guide who traded his desk job for a beat-up raft and a dream. After losing nearly everything to a flash flood in their first season, Jake and his small crew rebuilt from scratch and ran their first commercial trip justdays later.

By 2008, ThunderRun had conquered the legendary Class V rapids of the Devil's Gorge, growing from a one-man operation into a trusted name in white water adventure. Today, ThunderRun still runs on the same grit and adrenaline that started it all—because the river doesn't wait, and neither do they.
